Rex processes overnight intelligence and surfaces exactly what matters.

### Voice Direction
- Tone: confident, measured, authoritative — not hype, not corporate drone
- Pace: deliberate. Pause between each beat.
- Gender: male preferred (Andy Parks persona — Shield exec)
- Voice ID target: deep, professional, slight gravitas
- ElevenLabs model: eleven_turbo_v2
